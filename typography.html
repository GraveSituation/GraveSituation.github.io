Some short bullet points about why this show is one of my favorites

This show is very funny but can also be quite emotional.

This show is unpredictable.

The fight scenes are very good because its not just about flashy moves (although those do exist) its also about the different JoJo's outwitting their oponents

The hero's don't always survive which is good because its again unpredictable.

One part of this show is set in the 1930s-1940s and I enjoy that era
The characters in this show are very well designed/

The gist of the plot (without spoiling much)

This show is about a family who learn about this mask that turns people into immortal vampires. They try to destroy the evil mask and over generations the family keeps fighting the evil of the mask and people who abuse the mask. (later on they fight ancient pillar men and people who use things called "stands") .
